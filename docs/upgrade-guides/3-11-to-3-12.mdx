title: Upgrading from 3.11.x to 3.12
---

## Preparation

Version 3.12 introduces new fields. To properly migrate from 3.11 to 3.12 please migrate first to minor version 3.11.3

## Reasoning

In version 3.12 `orderSettings` were moved to `Channel`. For more info relate to https://github.com/saleor/saleor/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md
In case of not following the guide may happenned unexpected migration of `orderSettings` data.
